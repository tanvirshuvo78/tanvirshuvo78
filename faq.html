Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gamebench?

In this site, you can expect to find the latest info and data on the latest pc or console games. You can also review the said game you have played and give your ratings on a scale of 1 star to 5 star as per your taste, additionally you need not to sign up to give a rating or post a review. So enjoy.

If I have a problem, who may I talk to?

If there is an issue you wish to discuss with us, you are always welcome to contact us directly by submitting forms on the contact page. You can also contact the owner on Facebook; the link is given in the about us page if would so kind enough to visit

Can I download the games from this platform for free?

Not at all. This website is essentially an info database for the up and coming pc/console games. It is in fact not this site's any business to promote the links for downloading said games not to mention free. On top of that we do not support piracy as we might potentially face legal proceedings if we perpetutae this kind of behaviour.

How do I add a review?

All you need to do is go to the particular game page you want to add the review of. If you scroll down further, you will find a heap of reviews other users have posted on the site on this particular game. But above all the reviews, you will find a big box of two input fields: 1). Name 2). Add a Comment. So as per the self-explanataory functional names, you type your normal name on the first input, then write all you want about the game in the "Add a Comment" box and when you're done, you can happily click the post button. And thus your review on the game has been added

Are the ratings calculated based on the data given in this site or other sources as well?

No, the Average Rating on the games is purely calculated on the basis of ratings given by users on this particualer website: which is normally on a scale of 1 stars to 5 stars. Then we tend to average it out and return a consentaneous value
